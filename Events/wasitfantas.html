Teaser

Was it just my Fantasy

Was it just my fantasy? When I dreamt of being the most celebrated villain or the most powerful avenger? When I wanted to be a flame-breathing dragon? When I wanted to be the blushing bride?

We all live in delusion sometimes to escape the true reality. We’ve all stayed awake at night imagining fake scenarios in our heads. We all have the idea of perfectly imperfect characters, so here we spill the mirage of our fanciful notion and bring our characters to light.

The crux of this event is to express your never-ending visualization. Here you tell the world about the fictional friend you would like to have or be the fictional character you always wanted to be in your favourite book. A significant other you’d had in another universe or just an essence of your favourite conception.

The top 3 entries will get prizes worth Rs. 3000!!

Event Details:

  • Date : February 5, 2022
  • Time : 3:00 PM
  • Language: English and Hindi
